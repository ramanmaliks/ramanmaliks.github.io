Option strategies kehte hain woh techniques jinse traders stock market mein apne investments ko manage karte hain, boss. Yeh kuch specific combinations hote hain options ke buy aur sell karke, jinke through traders risk ko control karte hain aur profit maximise karte hain. 1. **Call aur Put Options:** Option strategies mein sabse pehle hota hai call aur put options. Call option kharidne se aap stock ko buy karne ka adhikaar rakhte hain ek fixed price par, jabki put option kharidne se aap stock ko bechne ka adhikaar rakhte hain ek fixed price par. 2. **Covered Call:** Yeh ek strategy hai jahan aap stock kharidte hain aur uske against call option sell karte hain. Isse aap premium earn karte hain aur agar stock price upar jaata hai, toh aapka loss cover ho jaata hai.3. **Protective Put:** Protective put strategy mein aap apne stock ke khilaf ek put option buy karte hain. Isse agar stock price neeche jaati hai, toh aapka loss protect hota hai. 4. **Straddle:** Straddle mein aap ek call aur ek put option ek hi strike price par buy karte hain. Isse aap market mein hone wale bade moves se profit kama sakte hain. 5. **Strangle:** Strangle strategy mein aap ek call aur ek put option buy karte hain, lekin alag alag strike prices par. Isse aapko profit kamane ka opportunity milta hai agar market mein volatility badh jaati hai. 6. **Iron Condor:** Yeh ek advanced strategy hai jahan aap ek call aur ek put option sell karte hain, phir unke higher strike prices par ek call aur ek put option buy karte hain. Isse aap range-bound market conditions mein profit kama sakte hain.Yeh kuch common option strategies hain jo traders istemal karte hain apne investments ko protect karne aur profit kamane ke liye. Lekin dosto, dhyan rakhein ki options trading risky ho sakti hai, isliye sahi knowledge aur experience ke saath hi trading karein. Arey bhai, options trading mein aur bhi kuch strategies hain jo traders use karte hain. Chalo, aur kuch bataata hoon: 7. **Bull Call Spread:** Ismein aap ek call option buy karte hain aur phir ek aur call option sell karte hain higher strike price par. Isse aap profit kamate hain agar stock price badh jaati hai. 8. **Bear Put Spread:** Iss strategy mein aap ek put option buy karte hain aur phir ek aur put option sell karte hain lower strike price par. Isse aap profit kamate hain agar stock price ghat jaati hai. 9. **Butterfly Spread:** Butterfly spread mein aap ek call option buy karte hain aur do call options sell karte hain, ek lower strike price par aur ek higher strike price par. Isse aap range-bound market conditions mein profit kamate hain. 10. **Iron Butterfly:** Yeh ek combination hai straddle aur iron condor ki. Ismein aap ek call aur ek put option sell karte hain, phir unke lower strike prices par ek call aur ek put option buy karte hain.In sabhi strategies mein, traders apne risk aur reward ko manage karne ke liye options ka use karte hain. Har strategy ke apne pros aur cons hote hain, isliye traders ko market conditions ko dhyan mein rakhte hue apni strategy choose karni chahiye. Samjhe boss?
