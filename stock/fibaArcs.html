Fibonacci

Fbonacci arcs ek tarah ka technical analysis tool hai jo traders use karte hain price charts par potential support aur resistance levels aur trendlines ko identify karne ke liye. Ye arcs curves hote hain jo draw kiye jate hain do points ke beech mein price chart par, jaise ki ek swing low aur ek swing high ke beech. In arcs ko Fibonacci ratios par based draw kiya jata hai.

Jab hum Fibonacci arcs ka istemal karte hain, to hum dekh sakte hain ki ye curves kaise market mein potential areas of support aur resistance create karte hain. Inka basic concept yeh hota hai ki jab price trend mein hota hai, toh market mein certain levels par price mein movement hoti hai. Fibonacci arcs in levels ko highlight karte hain aur traders ko help karte hain potential entry aur exit points ko identify karne mein.

Is tarah se, Fibonacci arcs ka use karke traders trendlines aur price action ko samajh sakte hain aur trading decisions ko improve kar sakte hain. Ye ek powerful tool hai jo market analysis mein use kiya ja sakta hai, lekin iska istemal karne se pehle traders ko iski understanding aur practice ko dhyan mein rakhna chahiye.
