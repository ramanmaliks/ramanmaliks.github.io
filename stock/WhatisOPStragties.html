Option Strategies Kya hai

Bilkul, samjhaate hain aur details mein:

  1. Call Option aur Put Option: Call option ek contract hota hai jismein buyer ko right milta hai, lekin obligation nahi hai, kharidne ke liye stock ya anya asset ko ek specific price pe specific samay tak. Put option mein bhi yehi principle hoti hai, lekin yahaan buyer ko right milta hai asset ko bechne ka specific price pe specific samay tak.
  2. Straddle: Straddle strategy mein investor ek call option aur ek put option ek hi strike price aur expiration date ke saath kharidta hai. Isse, investor ko market mein kisi bhi direction mein hone waale significant movement se profit hota hai.
  3. Strangle: Strangle strategy mein investor ek call option aur ek put option ek hi expiration date par lekin alag-alag strike prices par kharidta hai. Yeh strategy tab kaam aati hai jab investor ko lagta hai ki stock ya market mein volatility badhne wali hai, lekin woh specific direction mein sure nahi hai.
  4. Iron Condor: Iron condor ek advanced option trading strategy hai jismein investor ek call spread aur ek put spread combine karta hai. Yeh strategy volatility ke samay mein kaam aati hai jab investor ko lagta hai ki market range-bound rahegi.

In strategies ko samajh kar aur sahi samay par istemal karke investors apne risk ko kam kar sakte hain aur market ke movements se profit utha sakte hain. Lekin, har strategy ke saath risk hota hai aur isliye sahi research aur samajhdari se kaam karna zaroori hai.

Chalo, ek example ke through samjhaate hain:

Socho, tumhara ek stock XYZ hai jiska current price $100 hai. Tumhe lagta hai ke stock ka price next month upar jaayega, lekin tum certainty mein nahi ho.

Toh, tum ek straddle strategy istemal kar sakte ho. Tum ek call option aur ek put option kharid sakte ho, dono ka strike price $100 aur dono ka expiration date ek mahine baad.

Ab, agar stock ka price next month $110 ho jaata hai, toh call option profitable ho jaayega kyunki ab tum stock ko $100 par kharid sakte ho aur market price $110 hai. Aur agar stock ka price $90 ho jaata hai, toh put option profitable ho jaayega kyunki ab tum stock ko $100 par bech sakte ho jab market price $90 hai.

Is tarah, straddle strategy tumhe profit karne ka mauka deti hai bina kisi specific direction ki expectation ke. Lekin yaad rakho, agar stock ka price range-bound rehta hai, toh dono options expire out of the money aur tumhara loss ho sakta hai. Isliye, har strategy ke saath risk management zaroori hai.
