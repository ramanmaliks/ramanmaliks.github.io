Strangle

"Strangle" ek options trading strategy hai jo traders istemal karte hain market volatility ke samay mein. Yeh strategy option premiums ki volatility ke upar depend karta hai aur market mein sharp movements hone ki ummeed par based hota hai.Yeh strategy do tarah ke options contracts par based hota hai - ek call option aur ek put option. Yeh dono contracts ek dusre se alag strike price par hoti hain, lekin ek hi expiration date par hote hain. Yeh hai strangle strategy ka basic concept:

  1. Call Option kharidna: Pehla step hota hai ki aap ek out-of-the-money (OTM) call option kharidte hain. Iska strike price typically current market price se upar hota hai.
  2. Put Option kharidna: Dusra step hota hai ki aap ek out-of-the-money (OTM) put option bhi kharidte hain. Iska strike price typically current market price se neeche hota hai.
  3. Profit Potential: Strangle strategy ka profit tab hota hai jab market mein ek sudden sharp movement hota hai. Agar market price call option ke strike price se zyada badhta hai ya put option ke strike price se kam ho jata hai, toh aapka profit hota hai.
  4. Risk Management: Strangle strategy mein risk limited hota hai premium ke amount tak jo aapne dono options ke liye pay kiya hota hai. Agar market price dono strike prices ke beech mein rehta hai expiration date par, toh aapka maximum loss hota hai jo aapne premium ke roop mein pay kiya hai.Is tarah se, strangle strategy traders ko market ke volatile movements se profit kamane ka ek tarika deta hai.

    5. Lekin, is strategy ko samajhne aur istemal karne se pehle, traders ko options trading aur market volatility ke concept ko achhe se samajh lena chahiye. Aur hamesha yaad rakhein ki options trading mein involved risk ka pata hona zaroori hai aur proper risk management techniques ka istemal karna chahiye.
