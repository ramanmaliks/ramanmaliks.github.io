Open interest analysis


Open interest analysis ek important concept hai stock market mein. Ye batata hai ki ek particular option ya futures contract par kitne contracts khareede gaye hain, jo abhi tak khule hain. Jaise ki agar bahut saare log ek particular strike price par call option kharidte hain, toh iska matlab hai ki unka interest badh raha hai us price ke upar jaane mein. Is analysis se traders future price movement ka anumaan lagate hain aur trading strategies banate hain. Overall, open interest analysis market sentiment ko samajhne mein madad karta hai.



Open interest analysis market mein traders ko ek insight deta hai ki kitne log kisi particular option ya futures contract par interest rakhte hain. Agar open interest badh raha hai, toh yeh indicate karta hai ki market mein interest badh raha hai aur possible price movement hone ki sambhavna hai. Traders is information ka upyog karke apni trading strategies ko design karte hain aur market trends ko samajhne ki koshish karte hain. Open interest analysis ek important tool hai market sentiment ko samajhne mein aur trading decisions lene mein madad karta hai.
